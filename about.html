Delve into Our Tradition

Welcome to our world, where tradition meets play, and every toy tells a story

In the heart of India, nestled in Karnataka, lies the quaint town of Channapatna, often called the "Toy Town" of India. In a world increasingly driven by technology and synthetic materials, the charm of traditional, handmade wooden toys is a breath of fresh air. Renowned for its exquisite craftsmanship, Channapatna has been producing eco-friendly wooden toys for centuries. These toys, deeply rooted in Indian tradition, are not only a testament to the town's rich cultural heritage but also a beacon of sustainable living. Among the many brands that have emerged from this tradition, "LACHER" stands out as a pioneer in reviving and promoting Channapatna toys on a global scale, and in doing so, promoting sustainable living. We infuse this timeless tradition with a contemporary edge. Our purpose is simple yet profound: to guide children toward embracing responsibility through clever, sustainable wooden toys.

The Genesis of Lacher

Lacher's journey began with its founders' simple yet profound realization: the world was increasingly leaning towards mass-produced plastic toys that were not only harmful to the environment but also lacked the cultural richness and educational value of traditional toys. In an era dominated by technology and synthetic materials, there was a pressing need to reconnect with nature and heritage. This realization led the founders to Channapatna, where they were captivated by the town's age-old tradition of crafting wooden toys.

The founders, a group of environmentally conscious entrepreneurs and artisans, were determined to bring the timeless charm of Channapatna toys to the forefront. They aimed to create a brand that would honor Indian craftsmanship while also advocating for sustainability. Delve into Our Tradition

The art of making Channapatna toys, known as "Gombegala Ooru" in Kannada, dates back to the reign of Tipu Sultan in the 18th century. Tipu Sultan invited Persian artisans to train the local artisans, blending Persian techniques with local expertise. This fusion resulted in the unique style of Channapatna toys, characterized by their vibrant colors, smooth finish, and intricate designs. Our artisans, with their unwavering dedication, skill, and creativity, craft exquisite handmade products at our factory. Their commitment to their craft is evident in every production stage, from turning and carving to painting and quality inspection.

Making Channapatna toys involves several stages, each requiring high skill and craftsmanship. The primary material used is ivory wood, known for its durability and smooth texture. The wood is first seasoned to reduce moisture content, making it suitable for carving. Artisans then shape the wood into various forms using traditional hand tools. Once the basic shape is achieved, the toys undergo meticulous sanding to create a smooth surface. They are then dipped in lac, a natural resin obtained from the secretion of lac insects. This process, known as lacquering, not only gives the toys their glossy finish but also ensures their safety. The final step involves painting the toys with natural dyes derived from vegetable and mineral sources, making them safe for children

We are devoted to supporting our artisans and ensuring their well-being in times of uncertainty. Discover the timeless elegance of traditional craftsmanship through our beautifully handmade products, and join us in admiring and respecting the dedication of our artisans.

Lacher's Mission

(Reviving Tradition and Promoting Sustainability)

LACHER is more than just a brand; it is a movement towards a more sustainable future. We not only bring the beauty and craftsmanship of Channapatna to a global audience but also significantly impact the local community. Lacher's mission is to revive the traditional art of Channapatna toy-making and promote sustainable living. The brand is committed to preserving this ancient craft by providing local artisans with fair wages and sustainable employment. By doing so, Lacher ensures that the skills and knowledge of these artisans are passed down through generations, keeping the tradition alive. In addition to preserving heritage, Lacher is dedicated to promoting sustainability. The brand's eco-friendly toys use natural materials and processes that minimize environmental impact. By choosing Lacher Toys, consumers are making a conscious decision to support sustainable practices and reduce their carbon footprint.

At LACHER, we believe in the power of education. Through our website, social media channels, and community outreach programs, we aim to educate parents and children about the benefits of wooden toys and the rich cultural heritage of Channapatna. Our toys are designed to foster early development, social interaction, and a deep love for nature. By providing children with safe, non-toxic, and environmentally responsible toys, we aim to instill a profound sense of stewardship for the planet from a young age. This early exposure to sustainable practices is crucial in shaping children's values and can shape them into creative, adaptable, and environmentally responsible individuals. We offer a range of resources, including articles, videos, and workshops, to help parents make informed choices about the toys they buy for their children. Our educational content covers various topics, from the history and making of Channapatna toys to the environmental impact of plastic toys and the benefits of wooden toys for child development.

Lacher's Vision

(Educating and Inspiring the Next Generation)

Lacher envisions a world where traditional crafts and sustainable living are preserved and celebrated. The brand believes in the power of education and aims to inspire the next generation to appreciate and embrace eco-friendly practices. Lacher's vision extends beyond just selling toys; it is about creating a movement that fosters a deep connection with nature and heritage.

"We believe that small changes can make a big difference", and by promoting sustainable practices, we can contribute to the preservation and sustainability of our environment. Our journey is just beginning, and we are excited to see what the future holds. We are committed to continuously improving our products and practices, ensuring we remain at the forefront of the eco-friendly toy market. Through innovation, education, and a deep respect for tradition, we aim to inspire a generation of socially engaged and environmentally conscious children who will shape a better future for our planet.
